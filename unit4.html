Quick Notes (easy)

  • 3D transforms use 4×4 matrices and homogeneous coords (x,y,z,1).
  • Rotation about X/Y/Z axes are standard matrices — combine to rotate around arbitrary axis by change-of-basis.
  • Projections: Orthographic (parallel) and Perspective (depth effect).
  • View pipeline: World → Camera → Projection → NDC → Viewport.
